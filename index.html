About Me

All my life, I have been fascinated by technology. Since I was young, I have loved playing video games, spending time on the internet, and working with anything digital.

After high school, I worked many jobs. I had jumped between many blue collared positions and even spent three years in the infantry with the Canadian Forces. The entire time, I had been left feeling unfulfilled.

After my three year contract with the army expired, I decided it was time for a change. I knew that I needed to pursue a career that I could be passionate about. I began programming. I fell in love with it shortly after. The freedom to build and create whatever my mind desired was an exciting experience. Automating tedious tasks and building simple games became a hobby. The eureka moment that came when creating the perfect algorithm to solve a problem was so satisfying. Trying to create better, more flexible, and extensible code became an obsession of mine. I knew that I found what I wanted to do.

My experience as a developer is still in its early stages, but I continue to grow and expand my knowledge every day. I plan to keep developing my skills throughout my entire career. Becoming an absolute master developer is one my biggest dreams and aspirations.
